Compact Powder

apply and glow

Compact powder. Also known as Pressed Powder, is a semi-solid state of powder. Key ingredients being Silicones and waxes, Compact Powder adds a light texture to your skin-tone. Generally it is applied on top of the foundation to give a hold to the foundation layer.Regardless of the brand, always choose a shade that matches your skin color. If you end up picking up a compact that is a few shades lighter than your skin tone, your skin may turn grey or ashy in some time.
